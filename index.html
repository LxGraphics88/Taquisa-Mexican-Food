OUR STORY

Since 1995, Taquisa has been bringing the authentic flavors of Mexico to our community. Our recipes have been passed down through three generations, preserving the traditional cooking methods and genuine taste of Mexican cuisine.

Every dish is prepared with fresh, quality ingredients and the same love and care that our grandmother used in her kitchen back in Guadalajara.
